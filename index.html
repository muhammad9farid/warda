faq

Everything needed before you order

Questions about timing, customization, gifting in Egypt, and worldwide shipping are all answered here.

How long does a handmade order take?

Most single bouquets are prepared within 1 to 2 days. Larger custom pieces and event sets may take 3 to 5 days depending on complexity.

Can I request names, hearts, or custom colors?

Yes. You can request custom names, color palettes, ribbons, cards, chocolates, and gift add-ons during checkout notes.

Do you deliver in Egypt or abroad?

Yes. Warda by Sara is based in Egypt and can coordinate local delivery across Egypt as well as selected worldwide shipping.

How should I order if Instagram DMs are slow?

If Instagram DMs are delayed, use the checkout summary here or WhatsApp details so the order can still be confirmed smoothly.

What makes this different from fresh flowers?

Fresh flowers are beautiful for the moment. Warda pieces keep the gesture visible for much longer as handcrafted floral art.
